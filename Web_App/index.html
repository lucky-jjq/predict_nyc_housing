Data Analytics

We analyzed AirBnB listing data in Boston based on various attributes including number of bedrooms and bathrooms, districts of the listings, type of room, etc to arrive at an estimated nightly rate.

Methodology

The nightly rate is passed to our economic model. Together with the property price provided by the user, we canculate an internal rate of return to the investment.

Caveats

There are limitations to the model, including data points not captured by the model. For example, occupancy variance by districts and listing period, host profiles and ratings, other location-specific attributes, etc, are some of the factors that can impact the outcome.
